About

The Bharat Warriors are a dynamic cricket team built on passion, teamwork, and a warrior spirit. Representing strength, unity, and pride, we bring energy and determination to every match we play.

Our squad is made up of talented players who excel in batting, bowling, and fielding, but what makes us stand out is the bond we share as a team. We believe in playing hard, playing fair, and always respecting the game.

⚡ Motto: Play Hard. Play Fair. Play as Warriors.
